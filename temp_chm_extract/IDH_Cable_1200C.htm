Electrode connection (round connector)

Pin           Function                     Color (if applicable)

2           Reference electrode           White

3           Counter electrode           Red

4           Analog Ground

6           NC

 

Signal and Control Lines (15-pin D connector)

1          i1(A) = (Pin 1 reading - 2.5)(V) * Sensitivity 1 (A/V)

2.          i2(A) = (Pin 2 reading - 2.5)(V) * Sensitivity 2 (A/V) * 0.2

3          E = 2.5V - Pin 3 reading

4          RDE  (0-10V)

5          Ground

The digital control lines can be used to control stir, knock, and purge. Please check the manual of your cell stand for compatibility. Customized jumper settings or connection cables might be needed.
