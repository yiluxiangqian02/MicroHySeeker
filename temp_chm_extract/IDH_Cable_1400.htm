Cable and Connections

 

 

 

Electrode connection (7-pin round connector), Channel 1-2 and Channel 3-4

Pin          Function                              Color

1          Working electrode 1           Green; silver-colored cable

2          Reference electrode 1           White; silver-colored cable

3          Counter electrode 1           Red; silver-colored cable

4          Working electrode 2           Green; copper-colored cable

5          NC

6          Counter electrode 2           Red; coper-colored cable

7          Reference electrode 2           White; copper-colored cable

 

Each electrode leads connect to two channels.

 

Electrode connection (4-pin round connector), Channel 5-6 and Channel 7-8

Pin          Function                              Color

1          Potential Sensing 2           Green; copper-colored cable

2          Reference 2                    White; copper-colored cable

3          Potential Sensing 1           Green; silver-colored cable

4          Reference electrode 1           White; silver-colored cable

 

Each electrode leads connect to two channels.
