Hardware Specifications

 

 

Potentiostat:

  Potential Range:  ± 2.4 V

  Maximum current:  ± 2 mA

  Compliance Voltage: ±7.5 V

  Potentiostat rise time:  < 2 us

  Applied potential resolution: 0.003% of potential range

  Applied potential accuracy: ±1 mV, ±0.01% of scale

  Measured current range:  ±2 nA to ±0.002 A in 8 ranges

  Measured current resolution: 0.0015% of current range, minimum 0.3 pA

  Current measurement accuracy: 1%

  Input bias current: < 50 pA

 

Electrometer:

  Reference electrode input impedance: 1e12 ohm

  Reference electrode input bias current: <= 50 pA @ 25°C

 

Waveform Generation and Data Acquisition:

  Fast waveform update: 100 KHz @ 16-bit

  Fast data acquisition: 16-bit ADC, 20,000 samples/sec maximum

 

Experimental:

  CV and LSV scan rate:  0.000001 to 10 V/s

  Potential increment during scan:  0.1 mV @ 10 V/s

  CA and CC pulse width:  0.001 to 1000 sec

  CA and CC minimum sample interval: 100 us

  DPV and NPV pulse width:  0.001 to 10 sec

  SWV frequency:  1 to 5 kHz

  i-t sample interval:  minimum 50 us

  ACV frequency:  0.1 to 2 kHz

  SHACV frequency:  0.1 to 1 kHz

 

Other:

  Flash memory for quick software update

  USB port for data communication

  Power: provided by PC through USB port

  Maximum data length: 256K-16384K selectable

  Instrument Dimensions: 7" (W) × 4.5" (D) × 1" (H)

  Weight: ~2 lb.
