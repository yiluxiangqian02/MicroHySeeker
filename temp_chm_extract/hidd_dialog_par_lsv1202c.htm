Linear Sweep Voltammetry Parameters dialog box

 

In Linear Sweep Voltammetry (LSV), the potential is scanned from Init E toward Final E. The following diagram shows the potential waveform applied as a function of time. Current is recorded as a function of potential.

Notes

  Init E and Final E should be at least 0.01 V apart.

  When the second electrode is in Scan mode, it will have the same potential as the primary electrode throughout the experiment. The potential of the second electrode is therefore not independent.

  Linear polarization resistance plots can be generated using the Special Plots command under the Graphics menu.
