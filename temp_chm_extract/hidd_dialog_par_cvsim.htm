Parameter

Range

Description

Initial potential

Potential scan rate

Sweep Segments

1   -   1000000

Sweep segments (each is half cycle)

Sample Int. (V)

0.001   -   0.064

Data sampling interval

 

Notes

  High E and Low E must be at least 0.01 V apart.

  The system will automatically readjust High E and Low E values that are out of range.

  The system will automatically readjust initial scan direction to match the Init E, High E, and Low E values specified.

  When a large number of sweep segments is specified, the data sampling interval will be automatically increased up to 0.02V.
