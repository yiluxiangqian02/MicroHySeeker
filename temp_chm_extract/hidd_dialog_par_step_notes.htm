  A step will be ignored if its step time is less than 0.001 sec or shorter than its sampling interval.

  If the sampling interval for a Step sequence is larger than 0.002 sec, data will be transferred and displayed in real time.

  The sampling interval will be automatically increased if (step time * cycles / sample interval) exceeds 64K if data is to be transferred after the experiment.

  If the sampling interval is larger than 0.002 sec, data will be transferred and displayed in real time.
