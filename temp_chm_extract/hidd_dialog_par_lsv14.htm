Notes

  Init E and Final E should be at least 0.01 V apart.

  When electrodes are in Scan mode, they will have the same potential as the primary electrode throughout the experiment.

  For sequential measurements, the maximum scan rate is 5000 V/s, and data will be displayed in real time if the scan rate is less than 500 mV/s (or 3 V/s using fast com port speed). For simultaneous measurements, the maximum scan rate is 25 V/s and the maximum real-time scan rate is 100 mV/s (600 mV/s using fast com port speed).
