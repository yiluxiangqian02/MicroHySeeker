Simulation Variables dialog box

 

This dialog box allows you to specify selected CV simulation parameters:

 

Temperature

 

Enter the temperature in Celsius here. Both thermodynamic and kinetic parameters depend on temperature.

 

Electrode Area

 

Enter the area of the electrode in square centimeters here. The current is proportional to the electrode area in planar diffusion situations and surface reactions.

 

Capacitance

 

Enter the electrode double layer capacitance in microfarads here. Charging currents will be added to the total current reported during the simulation.
