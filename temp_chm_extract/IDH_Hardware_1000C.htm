Hardware Specifications

 

 

Potentiostat:

  8-channel potentiostat: 8 independent cells or 8 working electrodes in a single solution with common reference and counter electrodes

  Maximum potential: ±10 V for all channels, non-primary channels can be set to same potential as primary channel for potential scan and step

  Maximum current:  ±10 mA

  Compliance Voltage: ±12 V

  Potentiostat rise time:  < 2 us

  Applied potential resolution: 0.0015% of potential range

  Applied potential accuracy: ±1 mV, ±0.01% of scale

  Measured current range:  ±10 nA to ±0.001 A in 7 ranges

  Measured current resolution: 0.0015% of current range, minimum 0.3 pA

  Current measurement accuracy: 1%

  Input bias current: < 20 pA

 

Electrometer:

  Reference electrode input impedance: 1e12 ohm

  Reference electrode input bias current: <= 20 pA @ 25°C

 

Waveform Generation and Data Acquisition:

  Fast waveform update: 2 MHz @ 16-bit

  Fast data acquisition: 16-bit ADC, 1,000,000 samples/sec

  External signal recording channel at maximum 100Hz sampling rate

 

Experimental:

  CV and LSV scan rate:  0.000001 to 5,000 V/s Single channel, 25V/s maximum for 8 channels

  Potential increment during scan:  0.1 mV @ 100 V/s

  CA and CC pulse width:  0.0001 to 1000 sec single channel, 0.001 sec minimum for 8 channels

  CA and CC minimum sample interval: 1 us single channel, 100 usec for 8 channels

  DPV and NPV pulse width:  0.001 to 10 sec

  SWV frequency:  1 to 100 kHz single channel, 3125 Hz maximum for 8 channels

  i-t sample interval:  minimum 1 us single channel, 80 usec for 8 channels

  ACV frequency:  0.1 to 8 kHz single channel, 560 Hz maximum for 8 channels

  SHACV frequency:  0.1 to 5 kHz single channel, 280 Hz maximum for 8 channels

  FTACV frequency:  0.1 to 50 Hz single channel, 30 Hz maximum for 8 channels

 

Other:

  Potential and current analog output

  Digital input/output lines programmable through macro command

  Flash memory for quick software update

  Serial port or USB port selectable for data communication

  Cell control: purge, stir, knock

  Maximum data length: 256K-16384K selectable

  Instrument Dimensions: 14.2" (W) × 9.2" (D) × 4.6" (H)

  Weight: ~12 lb.
